01 Business Overview 8 citations +

Apple designs and markets smartphones (iPhone), computers (Mac), tablets (iPad), wearables, and services 10-K §1. Sold across 175 countries through retail, online, and carrier channels 10-K §1.2. The Services segment — App Store, Music, iCloud, TV+, Pay, Care — grew from 18% of revenue in FY2020 to 24.6% in FY2024 10-K §7.1.

03 Financial Deep Dive 18 citations −

Revenue declined 2% to $391.0B 10-K §8.1. Operating margin expanded 80bps to 31.5% 10-K §8.2. FCF of $110.5B (28.3% margin) funds ongoing buybacks. Services gross margin reached 74.0% vs. hardware 37.2% 10-K §8.3.

04 Moat Analysis 11 citations +

Network effects across 2B+ active devices create a durable switching moat. App Store generated $1.1T in developer billings, compounding platform lock-in 10-K §3.2. Brand premium sustains iPhone ASPs above $900 globally despite Android competition at lower price points.

06 Valuation 6 citations +

Trades at 28× NTM earnings vs. 5-yr avg of 22× — pricing in the Services mix-shift premium. DCF at 9% WACC implies fair value of $168–$192. Current price ~$215 represents a 12–22% premium to intrinsic value.

07 Bear Case 9 citations
China revenue at $66.9B (17% of total) faces regulatory headwinds and rising Huawei competition post-ban circumvention 10-K §1.4. DOJ antitrust case targeting the App Store 30% commission could impair the highest-margin segment. Vision Pro adoption remains negligible with no clear path to volume.
